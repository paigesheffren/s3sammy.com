About

Photos So Good You'll Hear The Engine.

Every car has a personality. I capture the energy behind every build with my work. My shoots are chill, creative, and I strive for bringing out what makes your build unique. The Experience

Swipe Through the Shoot

Here’s what it’s like to shoot with S3 Sammy — from the first DM to getting your final photos ready to post.

Swipe to see each step →
01 · Book

DM & Lock It In

Hit me up on Instagram. Feel free to add some info; your car, a few inspo pics, and when you’re free. We figure out the vibe, timing, and general area that fits your build.

02 · Plan

Vibe & Locations

We pick a route or a couple of spots — tunnels, rooftops, streets, or garages — and plan the kind of shots you want: rollers, statics, detail shots, or a mix.

03 · Shoot

Chill, Shoot, Drive

On shoot day, it’s laid-back and a vibe. We talk cars, move around a bit, grab different angles, and I guide you through everything so it never feels awkward.

04 · Edit

Turn Shots Into Visuals

After the shoot, I go through the photos, pick the best ones, and fully edit them — clean colors, sharp details, and that moody, cinematic look. Whatever you want.

05 · Deliver

Ready to Post

You get a gallery of high-quality files, sized for socials and screens. Post, tag, and enjoy — photos that feel as good as the car drives.
